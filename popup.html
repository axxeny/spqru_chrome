SPQRu, a Latin script for Russian language

An opinionated ruleset and automation by Arseniy Poroshin

This extension automatically transliterates Cyrillic text to Latin alphabet on certain sites.

There is only one ruleset currently, and it is the opinionated Latin alphabet for Russian language (carefully designed by trial and error over 15 years!) by Arseniy Poroshin.

In the future, the following features can be added here:

  • Choosing other rulesets, including for other languages.
  • Adding user-defined rulesets.
  • A textbox to transliterate what you write.
  • A textbox for reverse transliteration -- handy for e.g. typing in Latin and search in Cyrillic.

The extension currently works on the following websites:

  • Meduza
  • Russian Wikipedia
